**PLAY <span style="color:#FFAAAA;">*string*</span>**

Plays music as specified by *string*.  The string commands are:

- <span style="color:#FFAAAA;">*string*</span> - set of commands for the tone generator
  - A-G   - Notes
  - L     - Length
  - O     - Octave
  - P     - Pause
  - T     - Tempo
  - #or+  - Sharp
  - \-     - Flat

```ecb2
10 PLAY"L1;A;A#;A-"
```
